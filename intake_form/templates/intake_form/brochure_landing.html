Pre-requisites for Nutrition Consult
  1. 1Completed diet history form (filled by the pet parent)
  2. 2Completed medical history form (filled by your primary vet)
  3. 3For Wellness Nutrition consults: Blood reports (CBC, LFT, KFT), Stool and urine Reports: Validity of report- 3 months.
  4. 4For Therapeutic Nutrition consults: CBC, LFT, KFT and any additional tests as advised, Stool and Urine Reports: Validity of reports- one week.
  5. 5All diagnostic tests must be processed at a Veterinary Diagnostic Labs ONLY. Any follow up tests must be done at the same lab ONLY for better interpretation of your pet’s reports.
  6. 6Attendance of all persons involved in preparing or serving the pets meals is recommended at the in-person/tele/video consult.
Kindly Note
  1. 1We recommend that you reserve at least 30 minutes to complete the diet history form at home. All measurements of food which your pet consumes should be done on a digital kitchen weighing scale ONLY. The better the data provided to us, the better our recommendations will be to you. Thank you.
  2. 2Your primary care veterinarian is a vital partner in the care of your pet. The medical records specific to your pet must duly filled by your vet, must be sent and reviewed by us before making a dietary recommendation. To manage medical conditions, we must also work through your referred veterinarian to make nutritional recommendations. This is not optional. The more information we have, the better for the pet.
What to Expect
  1. 1You will be provided with relevant dietary information regarding your pet’s medical condition/wellness queries during the consult.
  2. 2Diet plans for individual pets is what we offer.
  3. 3Each nutrition consultation service includes the following: one in-person visit with your pet for a physical exam at a designated veterinary clinic, followed by an online video consult. Total Duration :1 hour; one follow-up video/tele consultation. Validity: within 3 weeks from the date of receipt of diet prescription. Duration: 15 minutes only; one nutritional formulation (i.e. one diet recipe prescription ONLY); immediate follow-up questions you may have about our dietary recommendations, food rejection or aversions by your pet are potential problems too. We provide additional guidance in these cases. Validity: upto 3 days post receipt of diet prescription/recommendations ONLY. Any appointment once confirmed will not be rescheduled.
  4. 4Your consult request, pet’s diet history and medical history forms and diagnostic reports will be reviewed before the online video consultation.
  5. 5Submission of ALL pre-requisites mentioned above is mandatory. Thereafter only, appointment for online video/tele consult will be scheduled and confirmed.
  6. 6The entire process (i.e. from submission of pre-requisites abovementioned to online consult) will be accomplished usually within 10 days only.
  7. 7Final diet recipe prescription will be emailed to the pet parent within 5 days from the date of online video consult.
  8. 8Availability of diet prescription on urgent basis (i.e. within 24-48 hours after in-person/tele-video consult) maybe provided but will be chargeable at an additional fee.
  9. 9You may check on the status of your consultation at any time by messaging at +91 9004097839.
  10. 10If you have other healthy pets in the same household, in most cases they are not identical to the first pet and will be considered as a separate second consultation chargeable service.
  11. 11All services provided will be by appointment ONLY.
  12. 12Any follow up consultation(s) in between (other than mentioned above) for in-person /video-tele nutrition consults will be chargeable.
  13. 13Fees once paid are non-refundable.
Pre-requisite forms will be emailed to the pet-parent only once FULL payment of fees is made for this service.
